About This Project

A bioinformatics pipeline for differential gene expression analysis in plant stress response, built to process and visualize large-scale RNA-seq data across rose and rice samples.

  • Automates cleaning, normalization, and analysis of gene expression data
  • Identifies differentially expressed genes under stress
  • Visualizes results with volcano plots, PCA, and heatmaps

Features & Tech

  • Python, Kallisto, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Jupyter
  • Handles 80,000+ gene expressions, 8 samples
  • Synthetic data generator for benchmarking
