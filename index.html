About Me

Hi, I’m Deetya, a computer science student focused on building strong fundamentals through consistent execution. I’ve worked on projects ranging from implementing algorithms like Dijkstra’s shortest path to building small applications and experimenting with modern development tools. I approach problems by breaking them down, testing ideas quickly, and improving through iteration. I focus on finishing what I start and building systems that work in practice, not just in theory. My growth has come from staying consistent, asking direct questions, and taking action even when I don’t have everything figured out. That mindset has helped me improve both my technical ability and how I approach challenges.

Through my projects, I aim to take ownership, communicate clearly, and contribute in a way that moves work forward. I value clarity, steady progress, and working environments where people can build without unnecessary friction. I focus on doing the work well, staying consistent, and building in a way that keeps things simple and peaceful.
